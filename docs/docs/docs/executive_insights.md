# Executive FP&A Insights – Target 2025 Outlook

## Overview
This analysis evaluates Target’s 2025 financial outlook using a base-case forecast and scenario analysis to assess sensitivity to demand and margin changes.

## Key Takeaways
- Revenue growth is relatively stable across scenarios, but profitability is highly sensitive to operating leverage.
- Downside scenarios have an outsized impact on operating income due to fixed-cost pressure.
- Upside scenarios demonstrate meaningful margin expansion driven by scale efficiencies.

## Base Case Outlook
- Assumes stable consumer demand and margin normalization.
- Implies steady revenue growth with modest operating income expansion.

## Upside Scenario Implications
- Incremental revenue growth translates disproportionately into operating income.
- Demonstrates strong operating leverage in the model.

## Downside Scenario Risks
- Flat demand combined with margin pressure significantly compresses profitability.
- Highlights importance of cost discipline in a lower-growth environment.
