# FP&A Scenario Assumptions

## Base Case
- Revenue growth: +2.0% QoQ
- Gross margin: 26.0%
- Operating margin: held constant at recent levels

## Upside Case
- Revenue growth: +3.5% QoQ
- Gross margin: 27.0%
- Operating leverage improves due to scale efficiencies

## Downside Case
- Revenue growth: 0.0% QoQ
- Gross margin: 24.5%
- Operating income pressured by fixed costs
